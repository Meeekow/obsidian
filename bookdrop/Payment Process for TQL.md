# Procedure
1. Navigate to [Link to Trax](https://trax.tql.com/accounts-payable)
2. Find and select any invoice that are age > 40.
3. Click `Pay Selected`.
4. Click `Make a Payment`, a pop-up will show asking to confirm, click `Yes`.
